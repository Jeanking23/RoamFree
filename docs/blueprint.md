# **App Name**: RoamFree

## Core Features:

- Accomodation Search: Unified search for hotel bookings and vacation rentals
- Integrated Transportation: Ride booking
- User Authentication: User accounts and profiles.
- AI powered recommendations: AI-powered suggestions for points of interest near the user's chosen accommodation. The AI tool can consider many factors, such as user reviews, ratings, and proximity to lodging.
- Interactive Map: Interactive map view showing available accommodations, transportation options, and nearby points of interest.
- Payment processing: Payment gateway integration.
- Rental Cars: Rental car booking

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A tranquil blue (#64B5F6) to evoke feelings of relaxation and trust, aligning with the travel theme.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F4F8) to provide a clean and modern backdrop, ensuring content readability.
- Accent color: Soft green (#81C784) for highlighting key actions and elements, promoting a sense of exploration.
- Body text: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), known for combining a modern look and some warmth and personality, making it well suited for readability.
- Headline text: 'Playfair' (serif), geometric with high contrast thin-thick lines, lending a elegant feel for the titles.
- Use simple, modern icons to represent different accommodation types, transportation options, and points of interest.
- Clean and intuitive layout with clear information hierarchy.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user experience and provide visual feedback.
